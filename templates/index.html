Welcome to our site!
Wage Daily is a site built by amateur coders, and is still in its beta stage, hence kindly excuse any errors that may occur
this site was primarily built for the welfare of the daily wage workers in our country. We aim to provide them with information of the available jobs in and around their area
By giving the workers information and providing employers a platform to find workers with, we can remove the middleman and effectively increase the income for the workers
